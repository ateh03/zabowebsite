FAQ

      

        

          
            What is Local AI / Edge AI?
            ▾
          
          
AI that runs on the device in your hand, not in a far away data center. Quicker answers with less energy, without compromising your privacy.

        

        

          
            Does Zabo need Internet?
            ▾
          
          
No. Zabo works offline, after initial setup. If you choose, it can use the web for extra context, but your private files never leave your device.

        

        

          
            Is my data private?
            ▾
          
          
Yes. Your data stays on your device. We don’t collect your notes, docs, or voice, nor resell any data. Period.
